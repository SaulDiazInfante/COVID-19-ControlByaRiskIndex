COVID-19 mitigation stratetgies: Controlling Risk by lightr traffic policies

Abstract

Argument: The number of individual in gathering could play an important role for control the spread of COVID-19. Thus by controlling the size of this group we can modulate the propagation speed. However, the restrictions of this kind has to be aligned with a delicate balance between the social and political implications. Thus epidemiologists and government consuls has to deduce a policy accordingly. That is, balance between the cost and profit of limit the size of meetings.
